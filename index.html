About Me

Hello, I'm Dr. Sebastian Calvache Rubio (MD, FRACGP), a passionate and dedicated medical professional originally from Colombia, where I completed my medical degree and developed expertise in Rural and Emergency Medicine. After migrating to Australia in 2014, I've had the privilege of serving vibrant communities on the Central Coast (NSW) and in Sydney. In 2022, I proudly became a Fellow of the Royal Australian College of General Practitioners.

Now settled in Perth, I have undertaken additional training in Adult General Medicine (Cardiology, Renal Medicine, Geriatrics and Rehabilitation medicine). I strive to make healthcare accessible and culturally sensitive for all patients. Whether you're dealing with everyday health concerns or complex conditions, I'm here to support you with empathy and evidence-based care.

I also provide consultation in Spanish.
